Just transforming matter into art

My name is Ra. I do art, I go to CIA currently.

I am an artist with a major focus in Illustration. In the past I called myself an allegorist over artists, for what I do is create stories in the form of images that narratively relate to deep and transcendental ideas about human existence. I look at myself as a playwrite attempting to craft ideas into images and stories to make difficult topics somewhat digestible. One way of looking at it is that I am a writer or poet that just happens to do art. So, my work leans to take on a otherworldly, abnormal, and even scary look... at least I aim for that.
